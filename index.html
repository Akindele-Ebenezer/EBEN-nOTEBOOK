    Eben Notebook GUIDE.
    To use this app, you should read the information below;

  1. B - BOLD. When you click on this button, your text becomes BOLD.
  2. I - ITALIC. When you click on this button, your text becomes CURSIVE/ITALIC.
  3. U - UNDERLINE. Click this button to underline text.
  4. INPUT BOX.Below, there is a box which you can use to input text, write your daily activities or jot down some important information. Scroll down to type in the box.
  5. DISPLAY BOX. After you type in your text/information, press any of the Buttons and it will display in the large box at the right.
  6. X - DELETE. Click this button to delete the current line of text in the right box.
  7. DELETE ALL. Click this button to remove/clear all the text/information in the right box.
  8. FONT SIZE. Click on the dropdown and adjust your text size to your choice.
  9. FONT FAMILY. Click on the dropdown and adjust your text style to your choice.
  10. TEXT COLOR. Click on the droopdown and adjust your text color to the colors available.
    More features will be added soon...
  11. A-. Click on this button to reduce the size of your text (default).
  12. A+. Click on this button to increase the size of your text (default).
